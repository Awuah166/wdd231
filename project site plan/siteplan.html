Site Plan

Site Name

Crebel Eatery

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about Crebel Eatery, a restaurant that offers a variety of delicious continental meals, beverages, and local dishes. The site will showcase the menu, location, hours of operation, and contact information for the eatery. It will also include a section for customer reviews and testimonials.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A potential customer visits the site to view the menu and decide what to order.

They navigate to the menu page, browse through the different categories of food and drinks, and select their desired items. They then proceed to the contact page to find the eatery's phone number and location for pickup or delivery.

Scenario 2: A customer wants to leave a review about their dining experience at Crebel Eatery.

They navigate to the reviews section of the site, read through existing testimonials, and then fill out a form to submit their own review. They provide their name, rating, and comments about the food and service they received.
